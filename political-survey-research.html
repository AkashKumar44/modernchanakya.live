Political Survey & Research

Modern Chanakya is helping political parties and candidates to win tough elections, by developing and implementing innovative research methodologies and research strategies tailored to the needs of the candidates and their constituencies and thereby providing them a winning formula with a set of actionable ideas thereby helping them to beat their competitors.

Owing to the dynamic nature of this field wherein each constituency is different, our approach to each constituency is also different and unique. We go all out to aggressively help our clients promote their goals, achievements and interests, in the best possible manner.

Not only do we provide insights which we achieve through our research data, we even help our clients implement them.Our USP is that, we offer strategic analysis and tactical recommendations based on our findings.

Political Survey and Poiltical Research Theory

  • Study of Sample – Under this process, our team aims to reach about 4 percent of the total voters in your constituency. This is a good sample, through which we can understand the electoral situation.
  • Opinion Poll – Under this process, our teams meet the voters and people of the area and try to understand their electoral mood. Opinion poll draws our attention to the major issues of our region, the opinion of the people towards the government and hundreds of other points.
  • Door To Door Campaign – Under this process, our team meets the voters and people of the area and tries to understand their electoral mood from house to house. Meet people and discuss many points and work on what they think about you.
  • Booth Label Research – Under this process a special study is done at all the booths of the constituency. Its main purpose is to read the electoral mind at the booth.
  • Contact with effective voters – In this research, our team conducts a campaign by taking contact with influential voters of the area.
  • Telephonic Survey – Through digital, our team works with the people of a region through regular time to make the campaign successful.
  • Importants of Social Media Plateform – In today’s time, digital medium is very important for any political campaign, so Modern Chanakya also helps your political survey through social media platforms.
